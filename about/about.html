BUT WHO ARE WE REALLY?

We are a team of highly skilled builders. By providing in-house training, holding weekly build-nights and encouraging friendship within the team, we are pushing the boundaries of what our team is capable of on a daily basis.

BUILDERS

Providing a platform for people to meet and become friends for life, we strive to keep our community intimate. We host yearly meetups with the staff-team to reinforce this bond, growing the sense of unity.

COMMUNITY

Our staff-team alone comprises over 10 different nationalities, ensuring that all languages and timezones are covered. We are always on the lookout for both staff-members and community members to expand our multi-nationality.

  1. CONCEPTUALISATION

    August, 2011

    Ninrogue and Jallut conceptualise a project called "Kingdoms of Alandor", an MMO-RPG in Minecraft. They are a sub-branch of the then popular Minecraft server "The Core".

  2. INITIAL DEVELOPMENT

    October, 2011

    Ninrogue and Jallut create the first and main storyline of the RPG. They also commence the first builds and create styles for different races.

  3. GROWING TEAM

    January, 2012

    A substantial team have joined forces with the two founders. A team of about 5 builders and 2 lore writers are now working on the project.

  4. GUUS JOINS THE BUILD TEAM

    February, 2012

    After being rejected for joining the main build team for "The Core", Ninrogue asks Guus to join the "KoA" team instead.

  5. THE CORE AND KOA MERGE BUILD TEAMS

    April, 2012

    The Build Team Manager from "The Core" makes the decision to combine the two build teams and overlap the responsibilities for both teams.

  6. NINROGUE AND JALLUT RESIGN

    January, 2013

    Ninrogue and Jallut leave the "Kingdoms of Alandor" team due to personal commitments, leaving Guus in charge of the RPG.

  7. KOA V2.0

    July, 2013

    The decision to re-build everything on "Kingdoms of Alandor" is made by the senior team of "The Core". This caused disruption in the build team and ended up in some builders quitting.

  8. THE CORE BUILD TEAM MANAGER RESIGNS

    September, 2013

    The Build Team Manager decides to leave "The Core" and subsequently "Kingdoms of Alandor" due to grievances with the senior team.

  9. THE CORE CLOSES DUE TO POPULARITY DECLINE

    December, 2013

    The Core experiences a decline in player base and is closed due to insufficient funds. Talks between the senior team continues.

  10. THE CORE REBRANDS TO DAWN OF ASMARA

    January, 2014

    The senior team decides to rebrand to "Dawn of Asmara" and start development on a MOBA-style game. Boaler is promoted to Build Team Manager.

  11. SENIOR BUILDER PROMOTIONS

    February, 2014

    Boaler promotes a builder and Guus to Senior Builder, second in command for the MOBA-style game and the RPG.

  12. BOALER RESIGNS

    November, 2014

    Boaler resigns due to personal commitments.

  13. GUUS IS PROMOTED TO BUILD TEAM MANAGER

    December, 2014

    Guus is promoted to Build Team Manager and continues to lead the RPG.

  14. MOBA DEVELOPMENT STOPPED

    January, 2015

    The development for the MOBA-style game is halted after being thoroughly discussed by the senior team.

  15. EMBALLINA JOINS THE BUILD TEAM

    February, 2015

    Emballina is scouted out by a builder, is asked to apply, and accepted to become a trial-builder.

  16. HUMAN CITY V2.0

    May, 2015

    The owner of the project instructs Guus and the build team to re-build the Human City.

  17. REALM OF LOTHIREDON

    June, 2015

    Due to grievances with the owner of the project, Guus decides to branch of on an independent route with Emballina joining Guus as an investor. Part of the build team follows. "Dawn of Asmara" is rebranded to "Realm of Lothiredon"

  18. EXPANDING THE TEAM

    October, 2015

    We increase efforts to recruit more builders, lore writers, developers and moderators.

  19. COMPLETION OF THE NEUTRAL CITY

    March, 2016

    The build team, Emballina and Guus complete the first city - the Neutral City.

  20. KITPVP IS RELEASED

    June, 2016

    Plobnob completes development for our first mini-game to try and scout for more builders - KitPvP. We release pictures of the arenas on social media.

  21. SPLEEF IS RELEASED

    October, 2016

    Plobnob completes development for Spleef, our second mini-game to scout for more builders, pictures were again released on social media. Our first large-scale dedicated hub was created.

  22. HUMAN CITY V2.0 UNDER CONSTRUCTION

    January, 2017

    The build team commences work on the Human City v2.0 with Guus planning and putting the city together.

  23. DWARVEN CITY STRUCTURES COMPLETE

    April, 2017

    All the buildings for the Dwarven City have been completed, ready for pasting.

  24. AFFILIATION WITH THE MCAU

    August, 2017

    We join forces with the Minecraft Architects Union and support their goals and aims.

  25. WORLDMACHINE + WORLDPAINTER

    January, 2018

    Guus starts messing around with World Machine and World Painter in order to create the RPG world itself.

  26. CONTEST ENTRIES

    April, 2018

    We start submitting contest entries on various websites; this helped us gain publicity on social media.

  27. OBJECT PACKS

    May, 2018

    We start releasing object packs, free to download and free to use. People use these in their private and public builds.

  28. FIRST COMMISSION

    December, 2018

    We are approached to complete a commission for EliteMobs. We delivered the commission, keeping close contact with the client and updating them regularly.

  29. HUMAN CITY V2.0 COMPLETION

    February, 2019

    The build team, Emballina and Guus complete the second version of the Human City, our most dynamic large-scale project to date.
