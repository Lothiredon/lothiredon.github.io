About Realm of Lothiredon

Founded in 2015, we are a big MMO-RPG project in development. It is our passion to create the best player experience possible within the RPG/Minecraft community, and by inventing new mechanics and building with incredible detail, we believe we can achieve this. Whilst we are still in development, the players can play minigames such as KitPvP, Skyblock and Spleef. We also have a variety of other activities available on the server. Furthermore, we are always on the lookout for new staff-members to support our amazing team. Navigate to the staff-section if you wish to apply!
